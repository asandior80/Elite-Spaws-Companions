About Us

Elite Paws & Companion is dedicated to providing loving, healthy, and well-socialized puppies to families around the world. We focus on responsible care, secure delivery, and professional service. Our experienced team ensures every companion is matched with the right family, offering guidance and support from adoption to delivery. Your pet’s safety and happiness are our top priorities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I order a puppy?
A: You can browse our puppies section, contact us via WhatsApp or email, and we will guide you through the process.
Q: How is delivery handled?
A: All deliveries are tracked, with secure vehicles and a unique tracking code for each order.
Q: Are the puppies vaccinated?
A: Yes, every puppy receives full veterinary care before leaving our facility.
Q: Can I see reviews?
A: Yes, our Reviews section shows families who have received companions.
Q: How do I track my order?
A: Enter your location and client location in the tracking section to get a code and map route.
